Who am I?

Hello, I'm Rose! I'm happy to be able to share a little bit about myself with you.
Below are some of the key things that make up who I am.

  • Actress

    I have a associates degree in occupational studies for acting. I got into acting because I love story telling, performing, and helping to create something beautiful and moving.
    Favorite Shows: Doctor Who, Adventure Time, Parks and Rec, and ATLA.

  • Programmer

    I fell in love with coding after doing a data science bootcamp. I love being able to create something that is interactive and functional. Most of my life has been center around tech from a young age and it's so cool to learn more and more about how it all works.

  • Gamer

    A lot of my free time is spent gaming. I typically play RPGs or simulation games, and I have a dream of some day making my own.
    Currently Playing: Dragon Age: Inquisition
    Favorite Game: Dragon Age
    *If you are a fan of the Dragon Age series, please hit me up. I need to gush so much 😭

  • Music Lover

    I am constantly listening to music. My favorite genres are rock, folk,indie or some combination of the three with pop thrown into the mix. I also love to sing, play the ukulele, and am learning the guitar.
    My Current Jam: Green Grass by Ellie Dixon

  • Philosophies

    My core values are understanding, compassion, balance and acceptance. Something I often ask myself is, how do I make the world a better place? Because, at the core of who I am, I'm a person who wants to make other people happy. I believe the best way to change the world is to change yourself. After all, you are the only thing you can control. So, I may not be able to solve every problem facing the world, but I can at least make sure that in my small corner of the world, I bring love and kindness.

  • LGBTQ+

    I am so thankful everyday for being queer. I can't even imagine who or where I would be otherwise. I cannot imagine I would be half as happy. Being queer forced me to me grow in ways I never otherwise would have. I learned to approach myself with acceptance and kindness. It made me rethink my world view and things I accepted as truth. It lifted off the blindfold that otherwise would have kept me blind and ignorant and miserable for my whole life.

  • Learner

    If money wasn't a concern, I think I would spend my life in school. I love knowing more about the world we live in and how everything works. I would love to go to school for philosophy, theoretical physics, film, writing. The lists goes on and on. I want to know it all!
    Currently Learning: Spanish and Guitar

  • Plant Mama

    There are so my lessons of life to be learned from plants. I started taking care of plants when I was in the depth of my depression. I had a couple of plants that had nearly died and I was able to bring them back to life. It inspired me to keep living. Even though my soul and mind was a wasteland, with time and care I could come back alive.

Projects

Luna

Luna is a personal life tracker inspired by various habit and mood trackers, all combined into a cohesive, easy-to-use platform. The goal of Luna is to provide users more flexibility with personalization while maintaining a clean and intuitive user-interface. Luna uses react with redux on the front-end to create an interactive and responsive web application. On the backend, this application takes advantage of the non-blocking architecture of express.js and the middleware and validation features provided by sequelize.

Artisan Alley

Artisan Alley is a e-commerce application modeled after Etsy created by myself and three other team members. This project uses flask for the backend and react for the frontend. Technologies used also includes: Python, JavaScript, CSS, HTML, SQLAlchemy, and Redux. Functionality includes three full CRUD features for products, reviews, and cart. As well as one partial CRUD for favorites.

Vacay Stays

Vacay Stays is a web application based on AirBnB, and also my very first full stack project! This was a solo project that had react frontend and express.js backend. Other technologies used in this project include: Javascript, Node.js, CSS, CSSGrid, HTML, SQLite3, PostgreSQL and Redux.
